DrinkWise

Project information

  • Subject: Business Analysis
  • Category: App design
  • Company: DrinkWise
  • Project date: September, 2020

  • The Problem: Whilst 87% of Australians acknowledge that excessive-drinking is problematic, only 16% of drinkers are self-aware of their harmful alcoholic consumption.
  • The Goal: To create a solution that inspires Australians to have a positive perception towards safe drinking and enact it.
  • The Solution: MyAlchie is a gamified alcohol-tracking platform that aims to inspire Australians towards an internalisation of positive views towards safe drinking.
  • 1) Drinker Quiz: creates self-awareness by sorting individuals into one of the four categories of drinkers, with Initiators and followers being the two most unsafe drinker types.
  • 2) The Alcohol Progress Tracker is an alcohol-tracking feature that allows users to set personal goals and track the amount of standards they drink by photo-scanning the drink.
  • 3) Rewards Shop: Sobriety achievements awards users in-app coins that can be used to buy virtual backgrounds and decor for their animal collectibles.
